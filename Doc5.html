New Jumanji Co-Op Game Announced

By John Bonelli
Published 2 hours ago

A new four player co-op game based on the most recent Jumanji movies has been revealed and is scheduled to release later this year.

A new co-op Jumanji game titled Jumanji: Wild Adventures has been announced and stamped with an imminent release date. The latest Jumanji game to be released was Jumanji: The Video Game in 2019 which was a third-person co-op game based on the two reboot films. While it did receive a PS5 upgrade in 2021, it largely flew under the radar as a basic movie tie-in.

After the Robin Williams-led original hit in 1995, Jumanji went on to turn into a larger blockbuster franchise in the modern era with two sequels, Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle and Jumanji: The Next Level, in 2017 and 2019 respectively. With Hollywood stars Dwayne Johnson, Jack Black, Karen Gillian, and Kevin Hart becoming the faces of the franchise, Jumanji has garnered newfound notoriety in the zeitgeist of popular mainstream cinema over the past few years.

Fans of the movies will be pleased to know about a new trailer that's been released for a co-op Jumanji game titled Wild Adventures, coming on November 3. The short trailer showcases the characters from the new films jumping, running and fighting through jungle and snow-dusted environments. Jumanji: Wild Adventures is a strange departure for the studio behind it, as it's being developed by Cradle Games, the studio responsible for the Souls-inspired sci-fi action RPG Hellpoint, which released in 2020.

The gameplay of Wild Adventures seems to offer a lot of variety. The trailer shows moments where characters are running away from lava or spiders toward the screen a la Crash Bandicoot. Other moments look like a 2D platformer or an arcade-style top-down beat-em-up. While it's unknown if Jumanji: Wild Adventures will be playable solo, there was a chunk of the 38-second trailer dedicated to showing single characters on screen running and flighting enemies.

It seems odd that a co-op Jumanji game such as Wild Adventures is releasing in 2023 without a major movie to back it up. It's been over three years since Jumanji: The Next Level and there have been rumblings about a fourth Jumanji film in the works. With both Welcome to the Jungle and The Next Level having been released in December of their respective years, it's possible that Wild Adventures' November release date is an indicator that the next unannounced Jumanji film is also aiming for this holiday season. If true, the movie announcement should be happening soon seeing that it's already June and major Hollywood blockbusters almost always have long marketing lead-ups.

Jumanji: Wild Adventures is coming on November 3 to PC, PS4, PS5, Switch, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X|S.
